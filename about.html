About

Web Application which takes user data and generates meal plan for them along with their recipes
**Context**

It has become difficult to manage and plan daily meals for the average citizen due to the modern busy lifestyle. Among the enormous data present in the internet it becomes very time consuming to find new recipes and get all details of that recipe in one place. - We will explore a way to meet the user's meal planning needs using the Spoonacular API.

**How to use**

  • fill the five input fields, `weight`(kg), `height`(cm), `age`, `gender` and `physical activity` level. The `physical activity` you have three options, `light, moderate and active.` - and after fill up click `submit` button
  • The meal-plan section you have see 3 meal cards, for breakfast, lunch and dinner respectively. - Each of the meal cards have see the following: `meal image`, `meal name`, `calories` of the meal and a `get-recipe` button.
  • The recipe section you have see 3 tabs - `Ingredients`, `steps` and `Equipment`.
    - The ingredients tab display the list of ingredients along with their quantity required for the recipe.
    - The steps tab display a ordered list of steps that need to be followed.
    - The equipment tab displays a list of equipment needed for the recipe.
